Macy's European Coffeehouse & Bakery

Whether you are looking for a lively flagstaff restaurant that serves great food or a top-notch coffeehouse with the ultimate cup, Macy’s European Coffeehouse and Bakery is the place you want to land. Locally owned and operated, Macy’s has been capturing customers’ hearts and loyalties for thirty-plus years.

Macy's European Coffeehouse & Bakery opened its doors in 1990, and has been a beloved part of the community ever since. We're proud to offer a welcoming and cozy atmosphere, delicious coffee and baked goods, and a commitment to quality that has kept our customers coming back for decades.
