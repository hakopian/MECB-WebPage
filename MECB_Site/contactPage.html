Meet the Owner!

Macy's European Coffeehouse & Bakery was founded by Macy Johnson, a passionate coffee lover and trained baker. After spending years working in the coffee industry and perfecting her craft, Macy decided to open her own coffee shop to share her love for coffee and baked goods with the community. She dreamed of creating a warm and welcoming space where people could come together to enjoy delicious coffee, fresh pastries, and a friendly atmosphere. With hard work, dedication, and a lot of coffee, Macy's dream became a reality, and today, Macy's European Coffeehouse & Bakery is a beloved local spot that attracts coffee lovers from all over the city.
